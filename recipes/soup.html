Cheesy Turkey, Potato, Carrot & Celery Soup

A heartwarming soup recipe balanced with protein and carbs.

This recipe contains celery, chicken broth, ground turkey, carrots, cheese, milk, onion, potatoes and some seasoning. It's easily whipped up in 25 minutes with minimal tools and skills.

Ingredients

  • 2 sticks celery
  • 24 fl oz chicken broth
  • 3/4 lb ground turkey
  • 5 oz shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese, Mexican blend
  • 4 fl oz whole milk
  • 1/2 medium yellow onion
  • 2 medium yellow potatoes
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • salt and black pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Wash, dry, and medium dice potatoes. Place in a medium bowl.
  2. Wash, dry, trim, and small dice celery. Add to the bowl with the potatoes and set aside.
  3. Preheat a medium saucepan over medium heat.
  4. Once the pan is hot, add oil and swirl to coat the bottom. Add turkey, salt, and pepper; cook, breaking the meat apart with a spoon, until browned and crumbly, 4-5 minutes.
  5. Meanwhile, peel, halve, and small dice onion. Add to the bowl with the potato and celery along with the carrots.
  6. When the turkey is done, add veggies to the pan; cook, stirring occasionally, until softened, 3-4 minutes.
  7. Add broth to the pan; stir to combine the soup, then bring to a boil over high heat. Once boiling, reduce heat to low and simmer, stirring occasionally, until veggies are tender, 12-15 minutes.
  8. Once the veggies are soft, add milk and cheese. Continue to cook, stirring constantly, until the cheese is melted and combined, 1-2 minutes more. Remove from heat.
  9. To serve, divide soup between bowls and enjoy!
